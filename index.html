Raphaël Levisse

I am a graduate student with a MEng in Mechanical Engineering from UC Berkeley and a MSc in Applied Mathematics from Centrale Paris. As a roboticist, I am very passionate about modern controls, probabilistic planning and developing environment aware autonomous systems.

Previously, in my MSc in Applied Mathematics, I have worked on developing precise control algorithms on several types of robots mainly a 24 motor robotic serpent and a prosthetic arm for mice commanded via brain-computer interface.

Out of robotics world, I am an avid pianist (17+ years), loving improvisation, and a high altitude volcano/mountain climbing enthusiast.
